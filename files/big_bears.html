Big Bears

Big Bears is our morning program for children ages 4-5. Classroom activities build on prior learning, and children's interests help shape projects and collaborative play. Teachers support each child's readiness for kindergarten across academic, social, and emotional development.

Overview

The Big Bear class is the morning session for 4- and 5-year-old children. Teachers create an environment that encourages curiosity, independence, and cooperative learning while helping each child prepare for the transition to kindergarten.

Classroom Goals

The nursery school ensures that our school environment is safe, healthy and conducive to children's exploration and independence. Our teachers encourage students to:

  • Support a positive self image.
  • Develop social competence.
  • Encourage independence.
  • Foster a positive attitude towards school and learning.
  • Provide opportunities for cognitive, social, aesthetic and physical growth.
  • Create a comfortable environment for exploring and embracing diversity.
  • Work cooperatively with each family.
