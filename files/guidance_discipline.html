Guidance & Discipline Approach

Washington University Nursery School uses a relationship-based, play-focused approach to guidance. Teachers help children build self-regulation, empathy, and problem-solving skills through daily routines, clear expectations, and consistent support.

How Teachers Guide Behavior

  • Build trust and confidence through warm, consistent relationships.
  • Model respectful communication and help children use words to solve problems.
  • Support collaboration, compromise, and conflict resolution in real classroom situations.
  • Use predictable routines and transitions to reduce stress and support independence.
  • Adjust activities and classroom supports when children need extra help.

Partnering With Families

Communication is ongoing through newsletters, email, teacher outreach, and conferences. Teachers share observations, discuss progress, and partner with families to support each child.
